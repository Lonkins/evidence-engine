## Key Observations

### Capacity-constrained learners underperform
Learners with more than 20 meeting or teaching hours per week (L-1002, L-1005) show lower
study completion and lower practice score averages. Their available study windows are narrow.

Recommendation: For high-load roles (teachers mid-term, multi-site managers), schedule
micro-sessions of 30–45 minutes during identified focus windows rather than 2-hour blocks.

### Practice score threshold predictor
In this synthetic cohort, learners who achieved 75%+ on practice assessments showed
exam readiness. Learners below 70% on practice scores are likely not ready to book.

L-1001 (61% avg) and L-1003 (55% avg, failed) are below threshold.
L-1004 (80% avg, passed) validates this pattern.
