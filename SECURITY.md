# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report security vulnerabilities **privately** — do not open a public
issue for anything security-sensitive.

Preferred: use GitHub's private vulnerability reporting. Go to the **Security**
tab of this repository and click **"Report a vulnerability"** to open a private
advisory visible only to the maintainers.

Alternative: email **tomprice13@pm.me** with the details.

## What to include

- Affected version or commit
- Impact and, ideally, a proof of concept or reproduction steps

## What to expect

- **Acknowledgement:** within 3 business days.
- **Triage & severity:** within 7 business days, with a rough remediation timeline.
- **Disclosure:** coordinated once a fix is available; we'll credit you if you'd like.
