## Note on HG-2025-004

This work was acquired on the gallery's behalf by Helena Voss in April 2025. Purchase price: €340,000 from a Zurich intermediary (vendor: private estate, identity not disclosed per vendor request). Provenance documentation includes:

- FARI (Flemish Art Research Institute) Certificate of Authenticity, ref FARI-2025-0387
- Belgian Ministry of Culture export licence, ref BE-CULT-2025-1141
- Chain of ownership documentation from 1987 (estate of Hendrik Maertens, Brussels) to present

A provisional sale agreement has been reached with a UK private buyer for £285,000. The transaction is subject to final provenance verification by the buyer's own advisers, expected by end October 2025.

**Curator's note:** Insured at £285,000 pending formal sale. Should the sale not complete, reinsurance at acquisition cost recommended.

## Status of HG-2025-004 (October 2025)

A review of the FARI certification for this work was initiated at Victor Holt's instruction on 8 October 2025. The nature and outcome of this review are not reflected in this inventory document as the review was ongoing at the date of this report.

Felix Drummond's insurance assessment on 14 October 2025 included this work. His preliminary verbal assessment, shared with Helena Voss during the appraisal visit, indicated "significant concerns requiring further investigation."
