## Initial Statement (09:00, 15 October 2025)

I arrived at the gallery at approximately half past five. I had been engaged by Meridian Specialist Risks to conduct a periodic valuation of the collection. Helena Voss received me and showed me around. Victor Holt was present for the first part and then went to his office.

I completed the physical assessment by about quarter to seven. I made notes in the reading room for about an hour. Just before eight Helena told me she was leaving. She handed me a set of keys and left. I continued my notes for a further twenty minutes or so and then left myself, locking the door behind me.

I went to dinner at Foragers in Islington — I had a reservation at half past eight. I had dinner alone and stayed until about ten past ten.

When I got back to my flat I realised my laptop — my assessment laptop — was still in the reading room. I couldn't complete the report without it. I took a taxi back. The driver can confirm this — I paid by card.

When I arrived at the gallery the front door was unlocked. I found that immediately strange. I went in, called out, and went back to the reading room first to get my laptop. I then noticed that the light in the office was still on. I knocked on the office door, it swung open, and I saw Victor at his desk. I went in and immediately saw that something was very wrong. I called 999. I didn't touch him or anything else.

---

## Supplementary Statement (14:45, 15 October 2025)

Regarding the time of my departure: I can now confirm from my Oyster card that I tapped in at Camden Town Underground at 19:52. My dinner reservation was confirmed by OpenTable and the restaurant placed me at my table at 20:35. I have provided both of these records to DC Mehta.

I want to be clear: I left the gallery at approximately 19:45 to 19:50. Helena Voss had left before me — she locked the door from the outside and handed me the key through the door while I was still in the hallway. I locked the door myself when I left five or ten minutes later.

Victor Holt was, to the best of my knowledge, alive in his office when I left. I did not see or speak to him after approximately 18:00 that evening.
