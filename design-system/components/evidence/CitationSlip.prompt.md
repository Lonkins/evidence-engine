A cited passage rendered as a pinned paper exhibit — the engine's receipt. Aged stock, halftone screen, pushpin, verbatim quote.

```jsx
<CitationSlip
  docTitle="Electronic Access Log"
  kind="technical record"
  quote="2025-10-14 20:47:33 | CARD_EXIT | HOLDER: Helena Voss (HV-0041)"
  onOpen={() => openDoc("09-security-log.md")}
/>
```

- Omit `onOpen` for a static (non-interactive) slip.
- Slips rotate slightly and alternate direction when stacked — let them sit at a jaunty angle, like real pinned evidence.
