Quiet stencil status chip for inline metadata.

```jsx
<Badge tone="azure" glyph="◉">Azure</Badge>
<Badge tone="brass">Foundry IQ</Badge>
<Badge tone="contradicted">Statement</Badge>
```

- **tone**: `neutral` · `brass` · `grounded` · `contradicted` · `silent` · `azure`
- Use for document kinds, engine-tap origin tags, and live/offline state. For a verdict, reach for `Stamp` instead.
