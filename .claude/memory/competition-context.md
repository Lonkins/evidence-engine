Competition is the Microsoft Agents League Hackathon, June 4–14 2026. Today is June 9 — **5 days remain** until submissions close June 14. Winners announced June 30.

**Why:** Time-boxed sprint. Every architectural decision must account for 5-day delivery window.
**How to apply:** Prioritise projects that can be scoped and demo'd within 4 days of build + 1 day of polish/video.

## Tracks (enter 1 or all 3)
- 🎨 Creative Apps — GitHub Copilot tooling
- 🧠 Reasoning Agents — Microsoft Foundry (multi-step reasoning focus)
- 💼 Enterprise Agents — Microsoft 365 Copilot

## Hard Requirement
All submissions **must** integrate at least one Microsoft IQ layer: Foundry IQ, Work IQ, or Fabric IQ.

## Judging (100 points total)
- 20% Accuracy & Relevance
- 20% Reasoning & Multi-step Thinking
- 20% Reliability & Safety
- 15% UX & Presentation
- 15% Creativity & Originality
- 10% Community Vote (Discord)

## Prize Structure
- Best Overall Agent: $15,000 cash + $1,468 extras = **$16,468 total** (stackable with category wins)
- Category prizes (Creative, Reasoning, Enterprise, Best IQ Use): $5,000 cash + $1,468 = $6,468 each
- Accessibility Award, Hack for Good, Top Student: $1,468 each (no cash, swag + credits)

## Submission Checklist
- Public repo with README
- Demo video
- At least one Microsoft IQ integration
- No confidential data
